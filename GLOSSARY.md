# Glossary

## Algorithm

A sequence of steps used to solve a problem.

---

## Data Structure

A way of organizing data.

---

## Pattern

A repeatable problem-solving strategy.

---

## Iteration

Repeating over data.

---

## Lookup

Finding information efficiently.

---

## Aggregation

Combining many values into one.

---

## Transformation

Changing every item into another form.

---

## Filtering

Keeping only the required items.

---

## Mutation

Changing existing data.

---

## Immutable

Creating new data without changing the original.
