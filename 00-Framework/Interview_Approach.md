# Interview Approach

A good interview solution is more than correct code.

## Step 1

Understand the problem.

---

## Step 2

Clarify assumptions.

---

## Step 3

Repeat the problem in your own words.

---

## Step 4

Discuss possible approaches.

---

## Step 5

Choose one approach and explain why.

---

## Step 6

Write the algorithm.

---

## Step 7

Write the code.

---

## Step 8

Dry run with an example.

---

## Step 9

Discuss time and space complexity.

---

## Step 10

Mention possible optimizations.
