# Learning Rules

These rules apply to every lesson in THE JS MIND.

---

## Rule 1

Understand before coding.

---

## Rule 2

Think in English before writing JavaScript.

---

## Rule 3

Recognize the pattern before choosing the method.

---

## Rule 4

Understand WHY before HOW.

---

## Rule 5

Algorithm before syntax.

---

## Rule 6

Dry run before execution.

---

## Rule 7

Explain your solution aloud.

If you cannot explain it,
you do not fully understand it.

---

## Rule 8

Every mistake is documented.

Mistakes are assets.

Never delete them.

---

## Rule 9

Never memorize.

Build intuition.

---

## Rule 10

Consistency beats intensity.

Study every day.
